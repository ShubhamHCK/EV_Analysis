Project Overview

Electric Vehicles represent a shift toward sustainable and intelligent transportation systems. Modern EVs combine electrical propulsion, battery storage, sensors, and data systems to create smart vehicles that generate valuable operational insights. Using Tableau dashboards, this platform visualizes EV charging behavior, battery performance, and vehicle efficiency to enable better decisions for city planners, fleet operators, and consumers.

Real-World Data Scenarios

⚡ Charging Pattern Analytics

Analyze peak charging hours, charging locations, and electricity demand across cities to support future EV infrastructure planning.

🔋 Battery Performance Insights

Track battery health, charge cycles, and real-world driving range to improve fleet management and vehicle reliability.

🚗 EV Model Comparison

Compare EV models based on range, efficiency, charging cost, and environmental impact to help buyers make sustainable choices.
